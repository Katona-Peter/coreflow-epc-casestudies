Notification System
Message Types:
Success: Actions completed successfully (e.g., comment submitted)
Error: Something went wrong that needs your attention
Warning: Important information or required actions
Info: General information about your actions or status
Comment Status Guide:
  • Pending: Your comment is awaiting approval
  • Approved: Your comment is visible to everyone
  • Unapproved: Comment is hidden from public view
